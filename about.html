About Me

Hi, my name’s Danielle Panetta and I’m a personal trainer.

I am passionate about health and fitness. I have have played competitive soccer, run a couple of marathons (one in a school uniform!), cycled across France and climbed a few mountains (including an arduous trek up Mont Blanc, France). I’m currently training for a triathlon and planning the next adventure.

For me, fitness is all about being able to do the things I love comfortably.

I also have an interest in nutrition and am currently studying for my nutrition qualifications as I believe exercise and nutrition go hand in hand.

I understand that inevitably everyone is responsible for their own health and fitness goals, but as a trainer I provide the education, structure and accountability to help people transform their lives.

I have previously coached multiple clients to lose over 10kgs, run half marathons reduce blood pressure and cholesterol and build physical strength, even succeeding where other trainers had failed.

I’m hold both a certificate III & IV in Fitness through FIA FITNATION, including certification for Older Adults training. I’m registered with Fitness Australia, and certified as a boxing instructor with ACE. I hold curent CPR & First Aid Certificates.
