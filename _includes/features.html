Livida Fitness is about making positive, lasting changes in your life. I believe that losing weight and getting fit is about more than just exercise. I can help you break down mental and physical barriers, to help you live your life the way you want.

More Than Just Exercise.

Accountability & Motivation

How many times have you said "I'll start tomorrow", but "tomorrow" never comes? Get serious about your goals and commit to them out loud. Let me give your conscience a megaphone.

Nutrition Support

Did you know that weight management is 80% nutrition? Whether you want to lose fat, bulk up, or maintain your current weight, nutrition is integral to hitting your goals. I will provide nuritional tips and support to keep you on track.

Education

Sometimes it's hard to know where to start, or how to sift through all the information out there. I will help you figure out what works for you, and give you the tools and knowledge to ensure you keep your healthy habits throughout your lifetime.

Emotional Support

Eating better and exercising is not rocket science, but many people struggle to maintain a healthy lifestyle. I can help you identify your limiting beliefs and coach you through your barriers, helping you achieve more than you ever thought possible.
